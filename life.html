The journey of Destiny

I started schooling in Kouankan Refugee School at the age of sixteen(16yrs) in 2003 amid the critical time of my life as a sick patient
Despite the sickness, I managed to dux the section that I was in with 94% average. I was given a double promotion to the third grade the following year.
I did not accept the double promotion that year due to some advices from family memebers. I maintained the second grade class the following year and I also
dux the school with ninty seven point two(97.2 average) at the same school in Guinea and I was given a double promotion. My mother then managed to send for me
thorugh one of my big sisters called sis Fatu Nyei.

I arrived in Monrovia in 2004 and I there after took an entrance at a school called Africa Islamic Center School
located in Jacob Town, Monrovia. I passed the entrance and I was admitted in the same years I accepted the promotion
that was given me from Guinea. I managed to dux the school again in the forth grade and I was given a promotion
at the end of the first semester. And at the end of the school year, I was promoted to the six grade class. I then asked
my mother to allow me live with my big sister in super market and my request was granted and I started living with them.

Whil in supermarket, I took an entrance for the six grade class at a school called Seku Ibrahim Sheriff, elementary and
Junior high school located in JJY Super Marker Gardnersville and I passed the entrance and I was also admitted . I started
schooling that in(2006). Based on my performance and according to the school hand book that year, any student attaining the average
85% and above will be promoted at the end of the first semester of the school and year. And my average at the end of the semester was around
90%, I cannot remember exactly. But the vice principal at the time called me in his office and inofrm me that I was qualify to sit the
the National exam with the ninhth graders and that I should inform my parents of the fees and I did and it did not materialize due to reason
to reason that I do not know. Actually I made some error in this explaination. This promotion issue came about after completing the six, and
seven and while in the eighth grade, that was when my vice principal asked me to inform my parents of my promotion to the next level and it turned
to be a dispointment to me. After spending three years in that school, completing the eight grade class and duxing my class until I finally dux the
the school in the eight grade . I was given a scholarship that year by the school administration and I decided to change my school to attend the Booker
Washington Institute in Kakata.

My decission to attend the Booker Washington Institute was greatly influenced by one of my big sisters son called Blama Sheriff. He encorage me to atted the
school because he was attedding there. My first semester at the Booker Washington Institute was a changelling one financially and academically. But as God could
have it, one of my brothers by the name of Abu S. Kromah agreed to sponsor me through out my stay at the dorm. I when underoll the first semester and my family organized
a little celebration for me in Kakata Margibi county. I spent four solid year at BWI and I graduated in september 15, 2012.
